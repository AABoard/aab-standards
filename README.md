# aab-standards
The AAB Standards repository hosts the open frameworks, taxonomies, and methodological guidelines developed by the International AI Assessment Board (AAB).

These standards aim to support consistent documentation, evaluation, and comparison of artificial intelligence use in educational contexts, including K–12, higher education, and workforce training.

The repository provides an open and transparent environment for researchers, educators, and practitioners to collaboratively develop and refine AI education standards.

Areas covered include:

AI literacy competency frameworks

AI education evaluation methodologies

Evidence Maturity Index (EMI) for AI education research

Case classification and taxonomy

AI learning outcome assessment guidelines

Responsible AI use in education

Contributions from researchers, educators, and developers are welcome through pull requests and discussions.
